Description

Spaghetti pie is a baked dish with a spaghetti noodle "crust" filled with layers of meat sauce, cottage cheese or ricotta, and mozzarella cheese, all baked together like a casserole or lasagna but in a pie pan. It's a comfort food that combines Italian flavors into a convenient and sliceable format, making it perfect for weeknight dinners or feeding a crowd.

This baked spaghetti pie recipe is an easy pasta dish. It is great for potluck dinners and freezes well.
